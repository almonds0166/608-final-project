**Beat Revolution** Our project Beat Revolution is a mix between the games Beat Saber and Dance Dance Revolution (DDR). Beat Saber is a virtual reality game where players hold “lightsabers” to hit incoming blocks to the beat of the song. DDR is a classic arcade game where players stomp or “dance” on directional arrow pads according to the given arrows on the monitor.
